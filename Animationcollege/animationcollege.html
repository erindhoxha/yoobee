Work

Animation College Level 5 NZQA Certificate

This course taught me how to think as a Designer, using different tools to reach the goal that you want to create digitally. Softwares we learnt were Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Animate & After Effects. Thanks to Leighton Browne for being an awesome tutor. :)

Assignment 1

Sketching ideas for a new logo & branding, which lead to create a new company logo. I chose Hardshell as my company as a Web Design agency, then created the business cards which we printed afterwards.

Assignment 2

Creating a presskit for the brand which we created from first assignment. Had to extend with new business cards, flyers, leaflets, website idea and social media platforms.

Assignment 3

Manipulation of images, colourising and fixing broken pictures to print them digitally correct. Taught about masking, retouching images & image montage which we added & brought life into static images.

Assignment 4

This assignment was about implementing the skill that we learned from third assignment, retouching an old image, and montage creating a new poster from a mixture and collections of different images, which lead to create 'Doggyman' and 'The I'.

Assignment 5

Presenting all the work that we've done past 3 months, which we had to present it in front of the class, discussing new ideas & learning about characteristics and principles of a good Graphic Designer.
